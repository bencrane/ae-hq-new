AccountExecutive.com — Product Identity Explorations

Five distinct visual identities for the career-intelligence platform, explored 2026-07-14. Each is a full, self-contained HTML mockup with realistic content (live AE roles, OTE comp, company-quality data, alumni career-path flows, and an AI ask surface). Open any variant to see it live at full fidelity.

Working direction at time of parking: a remix of C (application architecture — sidebar, ⌘K AI palette, job-table density) carrying B's typographic identity (serif display, ledger discipline) with A's data-delta language inside modules; D reserved as the skin for editorial/report surfaces. Not locked.

Variant A
The Terminal
Bloomberg-terminal-for-sales-careers. Near-black, dense data grid, monospace tabular numerals, amber accent, green/red headcount tickers, command-line AI ask bar. Maximum data gravity.
